About Samara

Born and raised in Dubai, Samara is a 22 year old recording artist who’s now mesmerizing Montreal, show by show.Her style of music is her “take on music,” and gives us glimpses of pop, soul, r&b and jazz. Samara thrives for her audience to feel each and every raw emotion that’s put into her music, by giving them the impression that they themselves are in on every story of each song.

In 2020, Samara self-released her first EP, The Art of Moving On. and her first single Moonlight
